Frequently Asked Questions

How do I save recipes?

Click the "Save Recipe" button on any recipe card or in the recipe modal. Your saved recipes will be stored in your browser and accessible from the "Saved Recipes" page.

Can I suggest new recipes?

Absolutely! We love hearing from our community. Use the contact form above and select "Recipe Suggestion" as your topic. Include as many details as possible about the recipe you'd like to see.

Are the recipes tested?

Yes! Every recipe in our collection has been thoroughly tested by our team to ensure it delivers excellent results. We provide detailed instructions and helpful tips for success.

How can I share feedback?

We welcome all feedback! Use our contact form, send us an email, or connect with us on social media. Your input helps us improve FlavorCraft for everyone.

Do you offer cooking classes?

While we don't currently offer in-person classes, we're working on virtual cooking workshops. Stay tuned to our newsletter for updates!

Can I print recipes?

Yes! When viewing a recipe in the modal, you can use your browser's print function to create a printer-friendly version of any recipe.
